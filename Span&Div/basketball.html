My Favorite NBA Players

Michael Jordan

Michael Jordan played for the Chicago Bulls and won 6 championships with them. He is considered one of the top players in NBA history.

Kobe Bryant

Kobe Bryant played for the Los Angeles Lakers and won 5 championships with them. He is considered one of the top players in NBA history. A lot of people say that his playstyle is similar to Michael Jordan.

Lebron James

Lebron James played for the Cleveland Cavaliers and won 4 championships. He is considered one of the top players in NBA history. He set the record for having the highest career points of 50k.
